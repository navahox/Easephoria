Simple process

A clear path from first call to a finished install—built for busy business owners.

1

Quick consultation + site check

We clarify your goal, take key measurements, and confirm what’s possible in your space (placement, visibility, cabling route).

2

Product recommendation + order coordination

Based on the site check, we specify what you need and coordinate the order with the supplier—so the right system arrives ready for install.

3

Installation & handover

We install, configure, and test the full system, then hand it over ready to run with simple instructions for uploading and scheduling content.
